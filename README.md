# Expert Pancake

A secure platform for managing personal and business email extensions with integrated social media publishing capabilities.

## Features

- Personal email management linked with business email extensions
- Social media integration and publishing
- Secure device backup and access management
- Multi-device session control

## Security Features

### Device Session Management

This platform includes robust security features to protect your account across multiple devices:

- **Session Management**: Ability to view and manage all active device sessions
- **Remote Logout**: Log out from all other devices when switching to a new device
- **Device Authorization**: Control which devices have access to your account

### Securing Your Account on Apple Devices

When using Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac), follow these security best practices:

1. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Add an extra layer of security
2. **Use Biometric Authentication**: Leverage Face ID or Touch ID for secure access
3. **Session Control**: When logging in from a new Apple device, you can automatically log out all other sessions
4. **Device Tracking**: Keep track of all devices that have accessed your account

## Managing Your Sessions

To secure your account when switching to an Apple device:

1. Log in to your account on the new Apple device
2. Navigate to **Security Settings** â†’ **Active Sessions**
3. Click **"Log out all other devices"** to end all active sessions except your current one
4. All other devices will be immediately logged out

This ensures that only your Apple device has active access to your account.

## Security Best Practices

- Regularly review active sessions in your security settings
- Log out from devices you no longer use
- Enable notifications for new device logins
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Keep your Apple device software up to date
